Stories from Our Kitchen

The Handi Tradition

Cooking in clay or heavy brass pots ('Handi') is an ancient Indian art. The porous nature allows heat to circulate evenly.

The Art of Spices

We don't use machine-ground powder. Our chefs use the 'Sil-Batta' to crush whole spices, releasing fresh oils.

Handi Mutton

Our signature dish. The meat marinates for 6 hours in yogurt and secret 'Khada Masala' before being slow-cooked over a wood fire.

Handi Chicken

Sealed with dough to trap steam ('Dum'), this dish cooks in its own juices, creating a succulent texture.
